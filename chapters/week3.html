4  Making Quaternary Data FAIR


4.1 Goal

Scholars of scientific data across many disciplines have engaged robustly with the project of FAIR data. This module surveys the response of Quaternary scientists to the FAIR principles.

4.2 Readings

  • Lannom et al., 2020: “FAIR Data and Services in Biodiversity Science and Geoscience”
  • Nicholson et al., 2023: “Will It Ever Be FAIR? Making Archaeological Data Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable”
  • The BiodiFAIRse GO FAIR Implementation Network

4.3 Questions

  • How have biodiversity scientists and archaeologists attempted to implement FAIR principles? What kinds of objects (e.g., individual datasets, databases, networks, etc.) have been the focus of their efforts?
  • Where have these scientists made the most progress? What are the biggest challenges that they face? Do they raise any concerns about Quaternary data that totally align with FAIR?
